Overview of Rarity Types

Common

The most frequently found cards—perfect for building your first decks and trading with friends.

Uncommon

Less common in packs, these cards introduce powerful Pokémon and Trainers to your strategy.

Rare

Shiny-stamped cards that add prestige to any collection and competitive decks.

Double Rare (RR)

Premium art and stronger stats—highly sought after by collectors and players alike.

Ultra Rare (UR)

Featuring holofoil and Legendary Pokémon, these are the crown jewels of booster boxes.

Secret Illustration Rare (SIR)

Beyond set numbers, full-art masterpieces with spectacular foil treatments—true collector’s gems.
