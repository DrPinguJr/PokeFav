Pokémon Card Rarity Types

Common

Common cards are the backbone of every Pokémon TCG collection. They appear most frequently in booster packs, making them ideal for building your first decks and trading with friends.

Uncommon

Uncommon cards offer a step up in power and collectibility. Found less often than Commons, they introduce new Pokémon or powerful Trainer cards to enhance your gameplay tactics.

Rare

Rare cards are your ticket to harder-to-find Pokémon and Trainers. With a shiny foil stamp, they are perfect for both competitive battles and showcasing in your collection.

Double Rare (RR)

Double Rare cards, marked “RR,” combine striking artwork with stronger stats or abilities. Highly sought after by collectors and competitive players alike.

Ultra Rare (UR)

Ultra Rare cards feature premium holofoil and often showcase Legendary Pokémon. Their limited print runs make them a prized highlight of any booster box.

Secret Illustration Rare (SIR)

Secret Illustration Rares go beyond the pack’s numbered set list, boasting full-art designs and spectacular foil treatments. True collector’s gems.
