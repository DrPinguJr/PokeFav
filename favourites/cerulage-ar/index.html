Deep Dive into Ceruledge’s Power & Legacy

This Illustration Rare art by Rond is among Surging Sparks’ most coveted pieces.

Lore & Trivia

Ceruledge was born when Charcadet fused with a cursed set of armor, imbuing its blades with the lingering resentment of a fallen swordsman. Its fiery edges scorch opponents with brutal precision, and its ghostly flame crackles with untold grudges—a perfect blend of flame and fury that makes this AR variant a true trophy for any collector.
