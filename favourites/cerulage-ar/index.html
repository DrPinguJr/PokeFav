Deep Dive into Ceruledge’s Power & Legacy

Abilities & Attacks

  • 🔥 Cursed Edge: Discard all Special Energy from all your opponent’s Pokémon.
  • 💥 Black Blaze Slash (160): Next turn, this Pokémon can’t attack.
  • ⚡ Weakness: Water ×2
  • ⛔ Resistance: None
  • 🚪 Retreat Cost: Colorless ×2

Release & Print Variants

  • ✔️ Regular print (Pokémon Co.)
  • ✔️ Ultra Rare full-art (5ban Graphics)
  • ✔️ Illustration Rare (Rond)
  • ✔️ Hyper Rare gold-etched (limited run)

Rond’s IR art remains one of Surging Sparks’ most sought-after pieces.

Lore & Trivia

Ceruledge emerged when Charcadet bonded with cursed armor, its flaming blades fueled by the spirit of a fallen knight. Its molten strikes and spectral embers make it a formidable foe and a prized narrative piece for collectors seeking depth and drama in their TCG lineup.
