Deep Dive into Garchomp’s Strengths & History

Abilities & Attacks

  • 💧 Tera (Water): Prevents all damage while on your Bench.
  • 🌊 Hydro Lander (160): Attach up to 3 Fighting Energy from discard to your Bench.
  • 🔊 Sonic Dive (120): Discard 2 Energy; ignores Weakness & Resistance for Benched.
  • 📜 ex Rule: Knocked Out → Opponent takes 2 Prize cards.
  • ⚡ Weakness: Lightning ×2
  • ⛔ Resistance: None
  • 🚫 Retreat Cost: None

Release & Print Variants

  • ✔️ Regular print (5ban Graphics)
  • ✔️ Ultra Rare full-art (5ban Graphics)
  • ✔️ Special Illustration Rare (Oswaldo KATO)
  • ✔️ Hyper Rare gold-etched (5ban Graphics)

The SIR full-art by Oswaldo KATO is the standout piece in Paradox Rift.

Lore & Trivia

This card showcases a Terastallized Garchomp wielding the Water Tera Type. In Paldea’s West Province, wild Garchomp can Terastallize into Water— depicted by swirling torrents around the dragon. Its mix of raw ferocity and fluid artistry makes this SIR variant one of Paradox Rift’s most coveted cards.
