Deep Dive into Garchomp’s Strengths & History

Abilities & Attacks

  • 💧 Tera (Water): Prevents all damage while on your Bench.
  • 🌊 Hydro Lander (160): Attach up to 3 Fighting Energy from discard to your Bench.
  • 🔊 Sonic Dive (120): Discard 2 Energy; ignores Weakness & Resistance for Benched.
  • 📜 ex Rule: Knocked Out → Opponent takes 2 Prize cards.
  • ⚡ Weakness: Lightning ×2
  • ⛔ Resistance: None
  • 🚫 Retreat Cost: None

Release & Print Variants

  • ✔️ Regular print (5ban Graphics)
  • ✔️ Ultra Rare full-art (5ban Graphics)
  • ✔️ Special Illustration Rare (Oswaldo KATO)
  • ✔️ Hyper Rare gold-etched (5ban Graphics)

The SIR full-art by Oswaldo KATO is the standout piece in Paradox Rift.

Lore & Trivia

This card showcases a Terastallized Garchomp wielding the Water Tera Type. In Paldea’s West Province, wild Garchomp can Terastallize into Water—depicted by swirling torrents around the dragon. Its mix of raw ferocity and fluid artistry makes this SIR variant one of Paradox Rift’s most coveted cards.

Competitive Role & Collecting Tips

Meta Impact: Garchomp ex SIR boasts “Tera (Water)” which can shut down aggressive Fire and Lightning decks by preventing damage while on your Bench. In Expanded play, its “Hydro Lander” attack accelerates Energy onto your entire back row, enabling powerful follow-up combos—especially when paired with Aqua Patch or Rare Candy to evolve quickly into other water-centric threats.

Deckbuilding Synergies: When building around Garchomp ex, include 3–4 copies of Professor’s Research and 2 copies of Boss’s Orders to dig through your deck and pick off vulnerable threats. Sea Garden can recycle key energies from the discard, making each “Hydro Lander” hit even more consistently. Tech in Crushing Hammer or Marnie to disrupt your opponent’s Energy strategy and maintain board control.

Collecting & Market Value: The Special Illustration Rare art by Oswaldo KATO is one of Paradox Rift’s most coveted full-art cards. As of mid-2025, Near-Mint Garchomp ex SIR #245/182 sells for around $12–$18 USD on platforms like TCGplayer and eBay. Prices spike after major set releases and tournament events, so monitor market trends if you’re buying in bulk or trading for profit.

Preservation & Grading: With its extensive foil coverage, Garchomp ex SIR is prone to edge wear. Always sleeve it in a soft penny sleeve before sliding into a rigid top-loader. For the best return on grading, aim for a PSA 9 or 10 by ensuring perfect centering, sharp corners, and flawless surface. Many collectors find a high-grade Garchomp ex is a marquee piece in any showcase binder.

Fan Community & Showcase: Garchomp ex SIR has inspired dozens of fan artworks and custom playmats featuring the dragon’s water-terastallized design. Share your own deck builds on forums like Pokémon TCG Online or Reddit’s r/pkmntcg—mention the #245/182 tag to join conversations around its strategic uses and stunning artwork.
