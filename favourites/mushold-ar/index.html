Deep Dive into Maushold’s Unique Traits & Story

Abilities & Attacks

  • 👊 Gentle Slap (50): Deliver a swift, precise strike.
  • 🐁 Gnaw Relentlessly: Put 1 damage counter on each of your opponent’s Pokémon for each Maushold you have in play.
  • ⚔️ Weakness: Fighting ×2
  • ⛔ Resistance: None
  • 🏃 Retreat Cost: Colorless ×1

  • ✔️ Regular print (The Pokémon Company)

Mizue’s Illustration Rare art brings Maushold’s family bond vividly to life.

Lore & Trivia

Maushold, the Family Pokémon introduced in the Paldea region, embodies teamwork and unity. In the wild, these creatures build sprawling nests where each member plays a vital role—mirrored perfectly by the card’s art, which captures their synchronized strength and unwavering loyalty.

Competitive Role & Collecting Tips

Standard Format Powerhouse: Maushold AR’s “Gnaw Relentlessly” ability is a game-changer in Standard play, dealing chip damage across your opponent’s entire bench each turn. When paired with draw engines like Professor’s Research , you can refill your hand and repeatedly re-use “Gnaw Relentlessly” to whittle down your opponent’s board state. It also synergizes beautifully with stacking staples like Boss’s Orders for targeted knock-outs and Path to the Peak to lock down Ability-dependent decks.

Deckbuilding Synergy: For maximum consistency, run 3–4 copies of Maushold AR alongside support Pokémon such as Frosmoth (to accelerate Energy attachments) and Dedenne-GX (to draw into your combo pieces). Consider teching in Crushing Hammer to disrupt your opponent’s Energy attachments, making each “Gnaw Relentlessly” hit even harder.

Collecting & Market Insights: The IR variant illustrated by Mizue captures Maushold’s familial bond with cozy warm tones—making it a fan favorite in full-art collectors’ circles. As of mid-2025, Near-Mint Maushold AR #226/193 trades between $5–$8 USD on sites like TCGplayer and Cardmarket. Prices tend to spike after major regional events when competitive players seek copies for their tournament lists.

Storage & Grading Tips: Because Illustration Rares have extensive foil coverage, surface scratches are the primary threat. Slide your Maushold AR into a penny sleeve before top-loading it, and consider a magnetic one-touch holder if you’re grade-submitting. When sending out for PSA or Beckett grading, aim for pristine centering and edge quality to maximize your potential grade (9–10).

Community Reception & Fan Art: Maushold AR inspired dozens of fan-created family portraits and animated loop art on social platforms like Twitter and Reddit. Trainers often share stories of how the card’s “homey vibes” rekindle their love for the game—making it not just a deck tech, but a cultural icon within the Paldea community.
