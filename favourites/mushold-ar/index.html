Lore & Trivia

Maushold, the Family Pokémon introduced in the Paldea region, embodies teamwork and unity. In the wild, these creatures build sprawling nests where each member plays a vital role—mirrored perfectly by the card’s art, which captures their synchronized strength and unwavering loyalty.
